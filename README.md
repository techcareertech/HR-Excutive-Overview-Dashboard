# HR Retention & Diversity Analytics Dashboard

![Dashboard Preview](https://github.com/techcareertech/HR-Excutive-Overview-Dashboard/blob/28cd3bf99c4a5974eae5d8e82a2ed17454fb0e54/Screenshot%202026-05-02%20093021.png)

## 📌 Executive Summary
This project analyzes a dataset of 1,000+ employee records to identify the drivers behind an **8.5% annual turnover rate**. By synthesizing demographic, compensation, and performance data, I uncovered critical retention "danger zones" and a significant pay gap affecting diversity. 

The final Tableau dashboard provides HR leadership with actionable insights to reduce attrition costs—estimated at **$500K - $1M annually** in reduced hiring and productivity loss.

## 🚀 Business Problem
The organization was experiencing unexplained talent drain in high-growth departments. Management lacked visibility into:
1. **Who is leaving?** (Demographic and Departmental breakdown)
2. **Why are they leaving?** (Compensation vs. Tenure vs. Culture)
3. **What is the cost?** (Impact on business continuity)

## 📊 Key KPIs & Insights
* **Overall Attrition:** 8.5% (Benchmark: 7.0% target).
* **Departmental Hotspots:** Marketing (12.6%) and Engineering (10.8%) show the highest turnover.
* **Diversity Equity:** Black employee turnover is **13.5%** (59% higher than the company average).
* **The "Danger Zone":** A tenure analysis revealed that 60% of Marketing exits occur within the **first year**, while Engineering talent exits after the **3-year mark**.
* **Compensation Gap:** Discovered an average **$8,600 pay gap** for Black employees compared to the company mean.

## 🛠 Technical Toolkit
* **Excel:** Data cleaning (handled nulls in `Exit Date`), outlier detection, and initial Pivot Table EDA.
* **Tableau:** Advanced DAX-style calculations, interactive filtering, and geographic mapping.
* **Analytical Techniques:** Cohort Analysis, Correlation Analysis (Salary vs. Attrition), and Trend Forecasting.

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations
1.  **Pay Equity Audit:** Immediate review of compensation structures to close the $8,600 ethnic pay gap and mitigate legal/retention risks.
2.  **Marketing Onboarding Overhaul:** Since 60% of Marketing staff leave in Year 1, implement a "90-day Buddy System" to improve cultural integration.
3.  **Engineering Retention Bonuses:** Launch a tiered bonus structure (5% at Year 3) to retain senior technical talent.
4.  **Stay Interviews:** Conduct proactive interviews for employees in the 2-3 year tenure bracket.

## 📈 Business Impact
* **Risk Mitigation:** Identified $2M+ in potential salary-related turnover risk.
* **Informed Decision Making:** Empowered HR with a self-service dashboard to track attrition in real-time.
* **DEI Roadmap:** Provided the data foundation for a formal Diversity & Inclusion initiative.
